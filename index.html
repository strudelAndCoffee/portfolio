About

I was born in Austin, Texas, and I have lived here my whole life.


My first job was bagging groceries at the Texas staple H-E-B. I started when I was 16, and eventually moved up to cashier, remaining a faithful employee for 7 years.


After finishing my Associates Degree in French at Austin Community College, I took an opportunity to switch jobs and work for another Austin staple, The Soup Peddler, first running home deliveries, then store commisary deliveries, finally working behind the counter as cashier and food prep at the original brick and morter. I am still working for the company, going on 7 years strong...


I have also found work and creative outlets as a musician, playing in bands and in the pit for music theater productions around town. Music has and will always be one of my biggest passions, but as a means of income, I have found that I am not cut out for the lifestyle, with the necessary hustle and financial uncertainty that comes with it.


For this reason, I have decided to welcome change and find a new passion to incorporate into my life - one that I can commit 100% to, and one with which to better support myself and my future family. I will always love music, and will continue to play as much as I can, but I can be just as devoted in time, ambition, and creativity towards a craft that is intellectually stimulating and highly marketable!


Thus begins my journey toward this new horizon, or as I like to think about it, in terms of another loved pastime of mine, Dungeons & Dragons: to level up! Level up my character, my life, my future prospects.


I hope that the work I have put on display here may demonstrate my potential for working in the diverse field of web development and programming.


Commitment, resilience, self-improvement, and the magic of collaboration are the core concepts that drive me to create the next new, the next useful, and in any way I can, the next beneficial for the world.
