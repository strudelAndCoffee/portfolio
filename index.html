About Stephen

A native Austinite, having lived here my whole life, I started my first job as many do: bagging groceries at the Texas super-market staple H-E-B as soon as I turned 16. Eventually moving up to cashier, I remained a dedicated employee for 7 years.


After finishing my Associates Degree in French at Austin Community College, I began studying audio engineering and recording, and switched jobs from groceries to pouring soups and making smoothies at another Austin staple, The Soup Peddler, where I have remained a dedicated employee going on 7 years strong...


I have also found work and creative outlets as a musician, playing in bands and in the pit for music theater productions around town. Music has and always will be one of my biggest passions, but as a means of income, I have grown weary of the financial uncertainty and level of schmoozing necessary to sustain work opportunity as a self-employed musician, especially in the changing landscape of Austin.


This is where I now look to a new craft to apply my self-motivated skills towards. One which <> passion to incorporate into my life - one that I can commit to 100%, and one with which to better support myself and my future family. Music will always be a pasion, but I can be just as devoted in time, ambition, and creativity towards a craft that is as intellectually stimulating and far-reaching (and highly marketable) as web development.


I hope that the work I have put on display here may demonstrate my potential for working in the diverse field of web development and programming.
