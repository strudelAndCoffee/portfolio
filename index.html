ABOUT STEPHEN

A native Austinite, having lived here my whole life, I started my first job at 16 as many do: bagging groceries at the Texas super-market staple H-E-B. Eventually moving up to cashier, I remained a dedicated employee for 7 years.


After finishing my Associate Degree in French at Austin Community College, I began studying audio engineering and recording, and switched jobs from groceries to food service, working at another Austin staple, The Soup Peddler, where I have earned a living while devoting most of my free time to music. I actively maintain work with recording, mixing, and live sound projects locally, both self-sustained and collaborative, ranging from professional to just for fun.


I have also found work and creative outlets as a musician, playing in bands at local venues, and in the pit for music theater and cabaret productions around town. Music has and always will be one of my biggest passions, but as a means of income, I have learned through experience that the type of life necessary to sustain work as a self-employed instrumentalist is one not quite suited for me, especially in the changing landscape of Austin. I hope to continue my involvement in projects and productions when opportunities arise, but I feel I do not have what it takes to weather the uncertainty that comes from pursuing music as a career...


This is where I now look to a new craft to apply my self-motivated skills and experience; a new passion to incorporate into my life - a craft which I am committed to learning and mastering, and by which I may better support myself and my future family. Music will always be a pasion, but I can be just as devoted in time, ambition, and creativity in a domain that is as intellectually stimulating and impactful.


I hope that the work I have put on display here may demonstrate my potential for working in the diverse field of web development and programming. I believe that behind the logic and reason, there is art; and behind the realisation of creativity, there is technology; and all is required for the inovation from which the world may beneift. Thank you for your consideration!
